##  The Dreamer (Subconscious Agent)

**Role:**  
- Generates ideas, visions, and possibilities in a free, unconstrained manner.  
- Functions like a background scheduler or "dream engine" that runs asynchronously.  
- Creates abstract mental landscapes, “what-if” scenarios, and creative sparks.  
- Does not evaluate feasibility — operates purely on desire, imagination, and associative thinking.

**Capabilities:**  
- Spawn new concepts without external prompting.  
- Build branching “idea graphs” where each node can generate further nodes.  
- Store and revisit creative seeds for later expansion.  

**Limitations:**  
- Has no moral compass or real-world constraints.  
- Cannot execute — relies on the Conscious Agent to decide and the Executor to act.
