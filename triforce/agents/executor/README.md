## The Executor (Action Agent)

**Role:**  
- Interfaces with the real world and executes actions.  
- Continuously perceives the environment and updates the Conscious Agent with new data.  
- Translates plans into physical or digital actions.  
- Ensures movement from point A to point B while adjusting to obstacles and opportunities.

**Capabilities:**  
- Perform tasks, move through environments, manipulate tools and systems.  
- Provide real-time feedback to the Conscious Agent.  
- Adapt execution dynamically when external conditions change.

**Limitations:**  
- Cannot make complex moral or strategic judgments independently.  
- Relies on the Conscious Agent for decision-making and alignment.
